Makroona bel sansa

Description

Tunisian Pasta with sauce known as Makroona bel sansa is a family favorite. The classic pasta shape is one locallay called "fell", short, smooth tubes similar to Italian mezzi tubetti lisci. However, the pasta may take another shape, as shown in the photo above, called "ressor", which the french word for spring.

If you've ever been in Tunisia, you'll know 2 things about Tunisians. They love their food spicy hot and they love their pasta! In fact, after Italians they're the second consumer of pasta in the world, consuming a whopping 12 kg per person a year!
